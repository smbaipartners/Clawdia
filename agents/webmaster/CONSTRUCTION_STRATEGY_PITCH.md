# CONSTRUCTION_STRATEGY_PITCH.md - SMB AI Partners

## 1. The Construction Vertical Pivot
**Core Thesis**: Construction is the ultimate high-leverage vertical for AI. GCs and Trades are drowning in manual "Toil" (Takeoffs, Estimating, Proposals) while managing high-value contracts.

## 2. Product Vision: The "Automated Estimator"
**Inspired by**: Togal.AI / Beam.
**Value Prop**: Transition from "Floor Plan" to "Professional Proposal" in minutes, not days.
- **AI Takeoff**: Automated quantity extraction from PDFs/Blueprints.
- **Price Engine**: Mapping quantities to material/labor rates.
- **Proposal Gen**: Professional, ROI-focused documents for clients.

## 3. Consultative Vertical: "AI for Builders" Classes
**The Hook**: "Stop Guessing, Start Growing."
**Offerings**:
- **Basics of AI for GCs**: ChatGPT for site logs, safety reports, and client comms.
- **Advanced Orchestration**: Setting up automated lead-capture and CRM syncing for trades.
- **The "Excel Killer"**: Migration paths from manual spreadsheets to automated estimating pipelines.
