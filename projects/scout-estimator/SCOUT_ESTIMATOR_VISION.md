## The "Tobo" Logic (Togal.AI Replication)
Togal.AI won because they made "Quantity Takeoff" a one-button experience. Our version—the **Tobo Engine**—focuses on the same high-velocity outcome but is purpose-built for the SMB contractor who doesn't need enterprise-level complexity.

### Core Architecture
1. **Intake**: Multi-format PDF/Image support.
2. **Vision Processing**: Using high-context LLM vision models to "see" the walls, fixtures, and legends.
3. **Automated Estimation**: Converting "Vision Pixels" to "Real-World Units" based on the detected scale.
4. **The "Tobo Proposal"**: Exporting directly to a high-conversion proposal template, skipping the Excel stage entirely.

### Key Differentiators
- **Simplicity**: No complex CAD setup. Drag, drop, and get your numbers.
- **Affordability**: Tailored for the GC who finds Togal.AI's enterprise pricing prohibitive.
- **Orchestration**: Integrated directly with the "AI for Builders" CRM pipeline.
